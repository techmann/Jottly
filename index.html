What is Jott.ly?

create nich communities connected globally
share ideas, news, and colloaborate with other chefs
create amazing dishes to
deliver to the foodies

Jott.ly started out as a chat room where cooks from a couple of restaurants used to share news, ideas, and collaborate without being in the same kitchen. Jott.ly has grown into one of the largest collaboration platforms for chefs. We serve 8 distinct communities around the world and connect over 700 chefs. We enable chefs to work together, regardless of location, and deliver to their foodies.

How does Jott.ly work?

Signing up for Jott.ly gives you instant access to chef communities and weekly video sessions from our top chefs. Start collaborating with chefs in an existing community or create your own. You can cusomize your home page to see the latest news and activity from the communities your’re a part of.
